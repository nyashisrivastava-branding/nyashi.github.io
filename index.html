about me

I live at the intersection of marketing and people strategy.

Brand marketer with 2+ years at Innovaccer, a healthcare SaaS company, where I built employer brand from near-zero. Grew social presence, created events that didn't exist, ran CSR programs across 9 NGOs, and won an AI hackathon by turning brand listening into a product.

I believe the best brands are felt before they're seen. My work sits at the intersection of creative instinct and operational rigour.

🎯 Brand is built in the details: the tone of a job post, the warmth of an internal newsletter, the energy in a room.
📊 Every creative call should be backed by data. Gut + metrics = campaigns that actually move people.
🌱 The brands worth working for treat their people like humans first, headcount second.

the magic.

01

02

Strategize

Build the plan. Messaging architecture, channel mix, content calendar, and a clear hypothesis for what success looks like.

03

Build & Execute

From design to delivery. Own the whole pipeline: vendor coordination, cross-team alignment, on-ground execution, quality sign-offs.

Measure & Iterate

Every campaign analyzed. Impressions, feedback scores, pipeline impact. Document what worked, sharpen the next one.

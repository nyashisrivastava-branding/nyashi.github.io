How I work

Method behind
the magic.

01

Research & Listen

I start by understanding the audience — what they care about, where they live online, what makes them stop scrolling. Data, feedback, competitor maps.

02

Strategize

With insight in hand, I build the plan. Messaging architecture, channel mix, content calendar, and a clear hypothesis for what success looks like.

03

Build & Execute

From design to delivery. I own the whole pipeline — vendor coordination, cross-team alignment, on-ground execution, quality sign-offs.

04

Measure & Iterate

Every campaign gets analyzed. Impressions, feedback scores, pipeline impact. I document what worked and sharpen the next one.
